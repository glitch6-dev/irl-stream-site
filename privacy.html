Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview
TG6 ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to it. By using this site, you agree to this policy.
2. Information We Collect
Information you provide: When you submit a contact form or notify-me request, we collect your name, email address, and message content.

Order information: When you place an order, payment and shipping information is collected by Stripe (our payment processor) — TG6 does not store your full card details.

Automatically collected: Standard server logs may include your IP address, browser type, and referring page. We do not run invasive tracking or sell this data.
3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to: process and fulfill orders; respond to contact form submissions; send notify-me emails when a product becomes available (only if you requested this); and improve the site based on aggregate usage patterns. We do not sell your information to third parties.
4. Cookies
This site uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some site features may not function correctly if you do.
5. Third-Party Services
We use Stripe for payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy) and Google Apps Script for contact form handling (subject to Google's privacy policy). These services may collect data as described in their own policies. TG6 does not control how these third parties handle your data beyond the scope of our integration.
6. Data Retention
We retain order records as required for accounting and tax purposes (typically 7 years). Contact form submissions are retained as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. Notify-me email addresses are deleted once you unsubscribe or once the product has shipped.
7. Your Rights
You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by TG6. To exercise these rights, contact us via the contact page. We will respond within 30 days.
8. Security
We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS for all site traffic and using Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure for payments. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take it seriously.
9. Children's Privacy
TG6 products and services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
10. Changes and Contact
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. For any privacy questions, contact us via the contact page or email dvelupr@proton.me.

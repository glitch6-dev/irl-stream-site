The Vision

No backpack existed that was actually designed for IRL streaming. You either rigged something together yourself, or paid thousands for enterprise gear that was way more than you needed.

TG6 fixes that. Three tiers — Metro, Roamer, Nomad — each built around the one question that actually matters: where do you stream?

Every product is tested live on stream. If it doesn't hold up on camera, in front of an audience, it doesn't ship.
